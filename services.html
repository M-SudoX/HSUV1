SERVICES OFFERED

The Health Services Unit offers the following:

  • Medical and Dental Examination
  • Referral to College Doctor/hospital if needed
  • Respond to Accident and Medical Emergencies
  • First Aid Treatment to minor injuries
  • Daily Weight Monitoring
  • Dispense Free Medication
  • Free Random Blood Sugar Testing
  • Infection Control and prevention of various illnesses
  • Environmental Hygiene
  • Immunization
  • Allergy Management
  • Medical and Hazardous Waste Management
