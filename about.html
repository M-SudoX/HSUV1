COLLEGE CLINIC

In accordance with HEI's Health Guidelines and Policies mandated by the Aurora State College of Technology, the Health Services Unit provides primary and preventive healthcare to ensure a positive health and safe environment.

The unit is properly maintained and accessible to sick individuals and persons with disabilities. It is well-equipped for quality care.

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

To provide medical consultation and primary health care treatment to college students and personnel.

To issue referral and convey students and personnel to higher medical institution during emergency situation .

To endorse outbreak of medical cases within the college vicinity to higher government health office for proper response care treatment. .

To take accountability and maintain confidentiality of all personnel and student health records..

Outbreak endorsement to government health offices.

Accountability and confidentiality of health records.
